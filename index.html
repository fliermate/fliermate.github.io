A Unicode codepoint converter

This Win32 console app will convert text content in input.txt and save output in output.txt.

Example input: "Insert your text content here, and get your converted font in output.txt."

Example output: "𝑰𝒏𝒔𝒆𝒓𝒕 𝒚𝒐𝒖𝒓 𝒕𝒆𝒙𝒕 𝒄𝒐𝒏𝒕𝒆𝒏𝒕 𝒉𝒆𝒓𝒆, 𝒂𝒏𝒅 𝒈𝒆𝒕 𝒚𝒐𝒖𝒓 𝒄𝒐𝒏𝒗𝒆𝒓𝒕𝒆𝒅 𝒇𝒐𝒏𝒕 𝒊𝒏 𝒐𝒖𝒕𝒑𝒖𝒕.𝒕𝒙𝒕."

Compile with FASM. Double click "unicode.exe" to convert, then open "output.txt" to see results.

BGGP 2024

In the past, I have participated in BGGP 2020, 2021 and 2023. What's going to be the challenge this year?

In BGGP 2023, I won PE category with smallest 1024-byte EXE. But in early 2024, I discovered a technique to tweak the header and further reduce the file size to 528-bytes.

However, I am not going to submit it with pull request (although the deadline is over). I am going to use it as PE template for upcoming BGGP 2024!
